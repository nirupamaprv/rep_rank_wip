# ReP Rank - Personal Branding Intelligence

## About

**ReP Rank** is a personal branding measurement and scoring platform designed for professionals and entrepreneurs. It provides an objective assessment of your professional reputation, benchmarks your brand against industry peers, and delivers a data-driven 30-60-90 day roadmap to improve your visibility and industry leadership.

### What is ReP Rank?

ReP Rank combines three core capabilities:

1. **Digital Brand Audit** — A comprehensive analysis of your online presence across LinkedIn, Twitter, Google Search, and your personal website to identify gaps and opportunities.

2. **Peer Benchmarking** — Objective comparison of your professional brand against industry peers to reveal competitive advantages and areas for improvement.

3. **30-60-90 Day Roadmap** — A customized action plan to systematically improve your ReP Score and achieve industry authority within 90 days.

### The ReP Score

Your ReP Score is a proprietary 0-100 metric that quantifies your professional influence and brand health based on:
- **Visibility (35%)** — Your searchability and reach across digital channels
- **Authority (40%)** — Trust signals, citations, and industry recognition
- **Engagement (25%)** — Quality of professional interactions and network influence

## Features

- 🔍 **Comprehensive Digital Audits** — Scan and evaluate your entire online presence
- 📊 **Competitive Benchmarking** — See how you stack up against peers in your industry
- 🎯 **Actionable Recommendations** — Get prioritized insights to improve your brand
- 📈 **Strategic Roadmap** — 30-60-90 day plan to build industry leadership
- 💡 **Objective Scoring** — Data-driven metrics, not vanity numbers
